Carrying capacity is 10 items + 1 item for every STR
Some items might count as 2 or more at the DMs discretion.
Items like pitons, lockpicks, arrows, crossbow bolts, and the like can be stored in bunches of 10
You can carry 1000 gold. No more.
##### Party inventory.
The party as a whole has a separate inventory, this is the things they drag behind them in big bags and on carts that are not available right away. The part inventory is accessible to everyone in the party. The DM keeps track of party inventory.

It's equal to 5 items for every party member.

The following items are exclusively stored in the party inventory:
- Food and water.
- Documents, like writs and permits.
- Sleeping bags. Tents.
- Quest items.
